Privacy Policy

Last updated March 4, 2026

Your privacy matters.

SEVN is built to help you discover things to do across the Vegas valley. We believe in keeping things simple and transparent about how we handle your information.

What We Collect

Your favorites are stored locally on your device using your browser's local storage. We do not create accounts, require logins, or store personal data on any server.

Location Data

If you grant location permission, SEVN uses your GPS coordinates to suggest the nearest area. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand general usage patterns such as page views and which areas are most popular. This helps us improve the app. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with anyone. External links within the app (event websites, venues) are governed by their own privacy policies.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach out at cuadventuresllc@gmail.com.
