Why SEVN exists.

We live here. Vegas is our city. Not the Vegas you see on a billboard or a 30 second ad. The real one. The taco shop that has been open since 4am. The trail with the view nobody posts about. The downtown art walk on a Thursday night. The neighborhood pool that somehow always has a vibe.

There is so much to do in this city that even locals miss out. We did too. We would fall into the same routines, same restaurants, same weekends. And every time we actually tried something new, we would say the same thing: "Why did we not do this sooner?"

SEVN is the answer to that question. A simple way to discover what is out there, keep track of what you love, and make the most of living in one of the most interesting cities in the world.

Why seven?

Seven is the number that runs through Vegas. The 702 area code. Lucky 7s on the slots. Seven days in a week to try something new.

Every time you open SEVN, you get seven curated picks for your area. Not a hundred options that overwhelm you. Not an algorithm guessing what you want. Just seven things worth your time, shuffled fresh every visit. Enough to spark an idea without the decision fatigue.

Built by locals, for everyone.

We are a small team of Vegas locals who got tired of the same "Top 10 Things to Do in Las Vegas" lists written by people who visited for a weekend. Every single activity in SEVN was picked by people who actually live here and have done the thing.

That said, this is not just for locals. If you are visiting Vegas and want to see more than the Strip, SEVN is for you too. We have 290 activities across eight areas, from the neon of Fremont Street to the alpine trails of Mt. Charleston. Locals tips included.

What we believe.

The best discoveries come from locals, not algorithms.

Every neighborhood in Vegas has something worth finding.
Free things can be just as good as expensive ones.
