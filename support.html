Common Questions

How do I save my favorites?
Tap the heart icon on any card in your SEVN list or inside the detail view. Your saved picks are stored locally on your device.
How do I install SEVN on my phone?
On iPhone, open sevnapp.com in Safari, tap the share icon, then "Add to Home Screen." On Android, tap the install banner or use your browser menu to add to home screen.
How does location work?
SEVN uses your GPS to suggest the nearest area in the Vegas valley. Your location data stays on your device and is never sent to any server.
Why do I see different picks each time?
Your SEVN is shuffled fresh every visit so you always discover something new. Tap "Shuffle a new SEVN" anytime for a fresh set.
Is SEVN free?
Yes. SEVN is completely free to use. No accounts, no subscriptions, no ads.
